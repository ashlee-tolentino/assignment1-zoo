Orca

The orca, also known as the killer whale, is actually the largest member of the dolphin family. Orcas are highly intelligent and social, living in family groups called pods that can number from a few individuals to dozens. They are apex predators, meaning they have no natural enemies, and they hunt cooperatively to catch fish, seals, and even whales. Orcas are found in oceans all over the world, from icy polar regions to warm tropical seas.
